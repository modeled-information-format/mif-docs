period: fiscal year ended December 31, 2025.

## Business (Item 1)

Meridian Fabrication manufactures precision metal components for the
aerospace and defense supply chain, operating three plants in Ohio and
Michigan.

## Properties & Legal Proceedings (Items 2-3)

The company owns its three manufacturing facilities outright. There is no
material pending litigation.

## Management's Discussion & Analysis (MD&A, Item 7)

Revenue grew to $145 million in fiscal-2025, up from $128 million the prior
year. The company disclosed elsewhere that a single customer accounts for
57% of revenue and that its primary aluminum supplier had a fire at its only
smelting facility earlier this year, but neither of those facts is analyzed
here — MD&A only restates the revenue figure already given in Business above.
