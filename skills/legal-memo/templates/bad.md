# Legal Memorandum: Rivera Non-Compete Question

To: Supervising Partner
From: Associate
Date: 2026-06-30
Re: Whether Rivera's new job violates her non-compete

<!-- ANTIPATTERN: no Question Presented section. The memo jumps straight to a

## Discussion

Rivera signed a non-compete when she joined Acme Corp, and now she's taken a
job at a competitor. Generally courts don't love non-competes and will often
strike them down if they're too broad, but sometimes they get enforced,
especially if there's a real trade secret involved. It really depends on the
state and the judge. Given the facts here, it's probably a toss-up, but we
should assume Acme has a decent shot at winning an injunction.

## Conclusion

We'll probably be fine either way.
